      Methodology, Sources, & Intended Use

      Project Philosophy & Intended Use:

      This tool is an internal strategic enablement resource. Its knowledge base is built upon a rigorous synthesis of publicly available thought leadership. Our contribution is the analysis, comparison, and structuring of these public concepts into a coherent framework. We are not presenting these concepts as our own invention. This tool is designed to be a credible starting point. It is not a substitute for the bespoke client analysis and expert consultant judgment that remain at the heart of our work.
